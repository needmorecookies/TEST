About Jotaro

The main protagonist of the third JoJo's Bizarre Adventure storyline.

Jotaro's characteristics

  1. He's super cool.
  2. He looks good in his iconic cap.
  3. He is introduced as a rough delinquent, but he has a gentle heart, and also loyal to those he likes.
  4. Star Platinum as his stand, one of the strongest stands in the series.

Jotaro's stand: Star Platinum

  • Close-Range Stand with incredible strength, speed, and precision.
  • Named after the Tarot card, The Star, which symbolizes optimism, discernment, and hope
  • Ora Ora Rush (突きの連打オラオララッシュ Ora Ora Rasshu, lit. "Barrage of Strikes"): Star Platinum's primary method of attack.
